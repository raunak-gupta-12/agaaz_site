Aagaaz

22th-24th Feburary,2022

ABOUT AAGAAZ,GCET
AN OVERVIEW
    Aagaaz'22 is a three day long event consisting of 18 competitions in various co-curricular activities.Commencing in IT Department,GCET in 2021; this is the second iteration of the event which is an opportunity to bring young people together in a controlled environment to compete against each other and to recognize each other's talents.The competitions are broadly cultural, technical, creative, photography, marketing and gaming oriented.
    Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology is yet again all set to celebrate its annual techno-cultural, arts and literary festival "Aagaaz". "Aagaaz" provides an opportunity to all the students to display their talents in various fields. The key objectives of such events is to unravel the hidden skills in each youngsters thereby boosting their self-confidence and self belief.
GENERAL RULES AND REGULATIONS
    All participants must ensure that they have registered for their respective event.
    Participants should be present during the opening ceremony as a walkthrough of the events will be provided.
    The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.
    Use of unfair means shall lead to immediate disqualification of the participant(s).
    Participants are expected to be respectful towards everyone. Any misbehaviour shall lead to disqualification.
    It is mandatory for all participants to be present for the Closing Ceremony, failing which they shall not receive their participation/ merit certificates
